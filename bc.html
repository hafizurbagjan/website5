Bangladesh Bank Circulars

Bangladesh Bank, the central bank of Bangladesh, issues circulars to provide regulatory guidelines, policy updates, and instructions to financial institutions operating in the country. These circulars ensure proper banking governance and compliance with financial regulations.

1. Types of Bangladesh Bank Circulars

a) Monetary Policy Circulars

  • Guidelines on interest rates and liquidity management
  • Inflation control and monetary policy adjustments
  • Cash Reserve Ratio (CRR) and Statutory Liquidity Ratio (SLR) requirements

b) Prudential Regulation Circulars

  • Loan classification and provisioning requirements
  • Capital adequacy and risk management guidelines
  • Basel III implementation framework

c) Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Compliance Circulars

  • KYC (Know Your Customer) and Customer Due Diligence (CDD) guidelines
  • Suspicious Transaction Reporting (STR) requirements
  • Compliance with international financial crime prevention measures

d) Foreign Exchange and Trade Finance Circulars

  • Regulations on import and export transactions
  • Foreign currency account management
  • Guidelines on remittance and foreign direct investment

e) Digital Banking and Fintech Circulars

  • Regulations on mobile banking and agent banking
  • Cybersecurity standards and data protection policies
  • Fintech innovation and digital payment system guidelines

2. Importance of Bangladesh Bank Circulars

  • Ensure financial stability and sound banking practices
  • Protect consumer rights and financial security
  • Prevent money laundering and financial crimes
  • Promote transparency and efficiency in banking operations
  • Facilitate economic growth and investment

3. Recent Developments in Bangladesh Bank Circulars

  • Updates on SME loan restructuring policies
  • Guidelines for sustainable and green banking initiatives
  • Enhanced cybersecurity measures for digital banking
  • Revised policies on foreign exchange transactions
  • Support measures for post-pandemic economic recovery
